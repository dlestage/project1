Cranberry and Vanilla Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 1/3 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp imitation butter flavor
  • 1 3/4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup sweetened dried cranberries
  • 2/3 cup white vanilla chips

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350F
  2. Spray cookie sheets with nonstick cooking spray
  3. In a large bowl, combine butter and sugar, beat till light and fluffy
  4. Add eggs and butter falvor; mix well
  5. Add flour, oats, baking soda, and salt; mix well
  6. Stir in cranberries amd vanilla chips
  7. Drop dough be tablesponnfuls 2 inches apart onto sprayed cookies sheets
  8. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes, or until edges are golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven. Cool for 1 min; remove for sheet to cool for 15 mins on wire racks
